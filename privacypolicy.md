# Gridoku Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 15, 2026

Gridoku is built by **Muhr Software**. We designed Gridoku to be a focused sudoku game without advertising, tracking, or unnecessary data collection.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Gridoku uses, how it is handled, and what choices you have.

## Summary

Gridoku does **not** show ads, does **not** use third-party advertising SDKs, and does **not** track you across apps or websites.

Gridoku may use Apple services such as iCloud, Game Center, StoreKit, and notifications to provide app features. Those services are provided by Apple and are also governed by Apple’s privacy practices.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Gridoku does not collect:

- Your name
- Your email address, unless you contact us directly
- Your precise location
- Your contacts
- Your photos or media
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing history
- Payment card details
- Data for third-party advertising or tracking

## Game Data Stored on Your Device

Gridoku stores gameplay information locally on your device so the app can work properly. This may include:

- Game progress
- Scores and statistics
- Awards and achievements earned in the app
- Online win counts
- App preferences and settings
- Daily free-game limit status
- Pause/resume state for an active game

This information is stored on your device and is used only to provide Gridoku’s features.

## iCloud Backup

If iCloud is enabled for Gridoku on your device, some Gridoku data may be backed up or synced through Apple’s iCloud services. This can help preserve your progress across your Apple devices.

The data synced through iCloud may include gameplay statistics, awards, online wins, and app preferences needed to restore your Gridoku experience.

Muhr Software does not operate its own cloud database for this data. iCloud data is handled by Apple under Apple’s privacy practices and your iCloud settings.

You can manage iCloud for apps in the Settings app on your device.

## Game Center

Gridoku uses Apple Game Center for achievements and online play.

When you use Game Center features, Apple may process information such as your Game Center profile, player identifier, display name, achievements, friends, invitations, matchmaking, and online match activity.

In Online Duel, Gridoku may use Game Center to:

- Invite a friend
- Match with another player
- Send both players the same puzzle map
- Compare scores
- Award online wins or related achievements

Your Game Center display name or profile information may be visible to other players during Game Center features.

Game Center is provided by Apple and is governed by Apple’s privacy practices and your Game Center settings.

## In-App Purchases

Gridoku Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase.

Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. Gridoku receives purchase entitlement information, such as whether Gridoku Pro is active, but Muhr Software does not receive your payment card details.

You can manage purchases and Apple ID payment information through Apple.

## Notifications

Gridoku may ask permission to send notifications, such as challenge-related notifications.

Notifications are optional. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the Settings app on your device.

## Diagnostics and Crash Reports

If you have chosen to share diagnostics with app developers through Apple, Muhr Software may receive crash reports or diagnostic information from Apple.

This information is used only to improve Gridoku’s stability and performance. It is not used for advertising or tracking.

## Support Requests

If you contact Muhr Software for support, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or a description of the issue.

Support information is used only to respond to your request and improve Gridoku.

## Children’s Privacy

Gridoku is a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

## Data Sharing

Muhr Software does not sell your personal information.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Gridoku uses Apple services, including iCloud, Game Center, StoreKit, notifications, and App Store distribution, where necessary to provide app functionality.

## Your Choices

You can control Gridoku-related data and permissions by:

- Disabling iCloud for Gridoku in iOS settings
- Signing out of or managing Game Center in iOS settings
- Disabling notifications in iOS settings
- Deleting the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
- Contacting us for support-related privacy questions

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
